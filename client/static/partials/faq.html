Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Sugar Rush?

People use the Sugar Rush to discover and order home-made desserts made by your communities' confectioners. Sugar Rush handles payment processing and offers a variety of home-made sweets near your home.

Confectioner Questions

How do I apply to become a Sugar Rush Patissiers?

We have a simple application process through the app to help you get started. Simply sned us an email to start! Our Head of Confectioner Relations will then order a dish from you, taste it, view your kitchen, and answer any questions you may have. If all goes well, you will go live on our platform and be able to share your desserts with our and your community. Refund requests, abuse reports, or bad reviews could lead to your removal from the platform.
